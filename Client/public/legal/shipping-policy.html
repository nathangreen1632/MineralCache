Shipping Policy

Last updated: October 15, 2025

Overview

This Shipping Policy is part of our Terms of Service and applies to any order placed through the MineralCache marketplace. Please review it before purchasing. Vendors are responsible for shipping their own items and for meeting the requirements below.

Shipping guidelines

  • Dispatch time: Orders must be shipped within seven (7) business days of payment confirmation.
  • Tracking: Vendors (and/or MineralCache, where applicable) must provide accurate tracking information.
  • Packaging: Items must be packaged securely to prevent damage in transit.
  • Listing clarity: Each listing must clearly state shipping cost, carrier/service, and delivery estimate.
  • Compliance: Vendors must comply with all applicable shipping laws and regulations, including accurate customs declarations for international shipments (if enabled in the future).

Wrong address

You are responsible for entering a complete and correct shipping address. If you submitted an incorrect address, contact us immediately at support@mineralcache.com. Address changes may not be possible after an order is processed or shipped.

Lost, stolen, or marked delivered

If tracking shows “delivered” but you did not receive your package, or if your shipment is marked lost by the carrier, promptly contact the carrier and notify us. MineralCache and vendors are not responsible for packages after a valid carrier delivery scan. We will assist with reasonable carrier inquiries or claims, but replacement or refund is not guaranteed unless covered by our Returns & Refunds Policy.

Delivery options & estimates

Typical options offered by vendors include the following USPS Priority Flat Rate services:

Service Price Estimated transit
USPS Priority Small Flat Rate $12.60 3–7 business days
USPS Priority Medium Flat Rate $21.95 3–7 business days
USPS Priority Large Flat Rate $31.40 3–7 business days

All delivery times are good-faith estimates and are not guaranteed. You will receive tracking information by email when your order ships.

International shipping

International shipping is not currently offered. Orders are limited to domestic U.S. destinations.

Other shipping restrictions

  • Not all services are available for all items or destinations.
  • Shipments to APO/FPO addresses may require additional processing time after delivery to the military post office.
  • Orders containing items from multiple vendors may ship separately.

Questions about returns?

For return eligibility and instructions, see our Returns & Refunds Policy.
