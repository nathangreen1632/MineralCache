Your Privacy Choices — We Do Not Sell or Share Personal Information

Last updated: October 15, 2025

Our commitment

Your data is yours. MineralCache will never sell anyone’s personal information—buyers, vendors, or admins. We do not “sell” or “share” personal information as those terms are defined under U.S. state privacy laws (including CPRA), and we do not use personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.

No sale or sharing

  • We do not sell personal information for money or anything of value.
  • We do not share personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising.
  • We do not allow our service providers to use your data for their own purposes—except where applicable for audit purposes.

Service providers and processors

We use service providers (for example, payments, hosting, email) under contracts that restrict their use of personal information to performing services for us. For vendor storefront operations, we act as a processor under our Data Processing Addendum.

Sensitive personal information

We do not use or disclose sensitive personal information to infer characteristics or for unrelated purposes. If that ever changes, we will update this page and provide required choices.

Global Privacy Control (GPC)

We honor applicable browser signals such as GPC. Today, because we do not sell or share personal information, there is nothing for GPC to opt out of on our site.

Your privacy rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or obtain a copy of your personal information. To exercise these rights, contact us using the methods below. We will verify your request and respond as required by law.

  • Email: privacy@mineralcache.com
  • Support: mineralcache.com/contact

Changes

If our practices change, we will update this page and our Privacy Policy. If we ever begin selling or sharing personal information, we will provide a clear opt-out link and honor your choices.
